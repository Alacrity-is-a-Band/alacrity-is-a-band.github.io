What does EDM sound like on a piano?

Alacrity is a Band is a musical duo of an experienced electronic music producer and a skilled piano player.






We take our musical style from experimental music, classical music, and many styles of EDM inclduing Drum and Bass, Dubstep, and Drift Phonk.

We use a variety of real life sounds and instruments while also doing some of our work in DAWs like FL Studio to take advantage of the powerful synths at our disposal. We use music notation software like MuseScore to compose our music that is played on real instruments.
